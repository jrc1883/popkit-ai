## How to Submit Feedback

1. **Choose the appropriate template** above
2. **Copy the template** to your clipboard
3. **Create a new issue**: https://github.com/jrc1883/popkit/issues/new
4. **Paste the template** and fill it out
5. **Add appropriate labels**:
   - `beta-feedback` (always)
   - `bug`, `enhancement`, `documentation`, `question` (as appropriate)
   - `critical`, `high-priority`, `low-priority` (for severity)

---

## Feedback Response Time

We aim to respond to all beta feedback within:
- **Critical bugs**: 24 hours
- **High priority**: 3 business days
- **Medium priority**: 1 week
- **Low priority**: 2 weeks
